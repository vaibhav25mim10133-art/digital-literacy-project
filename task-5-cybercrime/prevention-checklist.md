Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for College StudentsFinancial Safety: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required to send money or check your balance.Financial Safety: Ignore and decline unexpected payment requests on apps like GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm from unknown numbers.Download software, games, and apps only from official stores or verified publishers to avoid hidden malware or ransomware.Use strong, unique passwords for all college, email, and personal accounts, and always enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).Avoid logging into sensitive accounts or making financial transactions over free, public campus Wi-Fi networks.Verify the identity of anyone asking for urgent financial help via social media or messaging apps by calling them directly before sending funds.Regularly update your laptop's operating system and antivirus software to patch security vulnerabilities.Report Incidents Immediately: If you fall victim to a cybercrime, report it instantly on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930.
